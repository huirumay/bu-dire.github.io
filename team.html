meet the team

All Boston University students (undergraduate, graduate, part-time, full-time) are eligible for membership. In accordance with University policies, there will be no discrimination on the basis of race, religion, political views, sexual orientation, nationality, handicap, sex, etc. Members are required to attend all general member meetings and participate in at least one relief event each semester. The official roster of the student group will be monitored and updated by the vice-president of internal and external relations.

What's this about?

DIRE, short for Domestic and International Relief Efforts, is a disaster and humanitarian relief organization aimed at providing local and international relief during times of disaster including but not limited to seasonal and political crises.
